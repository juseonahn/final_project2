Programs

Educators


Our education programs are hands-on, collaborative, and impactful, taking inspiration from Warhol’s life and singular approach to making art.

Community


We strive to be a vital resource for the greater Pittsburgh community through a diverse network of programs and partnerships.

Teens


We work with teens to foster leadership through creativity, taking a cue from Warhol’s art and practice.

Series


We offer a range of series programs, including the ever-popular Sound Series concerts, a robust set of access events and activities, and inspiring artist installations.
